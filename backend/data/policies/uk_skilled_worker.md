# UK Skilled Worker Visa - Immigration Policy Guide

## Overview
The Skilled Worker visa is the UK's main work immigration route post-Brexit. It operates on a points-based system where applicants must score 70 points to qualify.

## Points Breakdown

### Mandatory Points (50 required)
| Requirement | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| Job offer from licensed sponsor | 20 |
| Job at appropriate skill level (RQF 3+) | 20 |
| English language ability (B1) | 10 |

### Tradeable Points (20 additional required)
| Requirement | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| Salary £26,200+ or going rate | 20 |
| Salary £23,580-£26,199 (specific criteria) | 10 |
| PhD relevant to job | 10 |
| PhD in STEM subject | 20 |
| Job on Shortage Occupation List | 20 |
| New entrant to labor market | 20* |

*New entrant allows lower salary threshold but caps at 4 years

## Salary Requirements (2024)

### General Thresholds
- Minimum: £26,200/year OR the going rate for occupation (whichever higher)
- Hourly minimum: £10.75/hour

### Reduced Thresholds Apply If:
- Shortage occupation (80% of going rate)
- PhD holder (80% of going rate)
- New entrant (70% of going rate)
- Specific healthcare/education roles

## Shortage Occupation List
Jobs on this list have advantages:
- Lower salary threshold (80% of going rate)
- Lower visa fees
- Points toward 70-point threshold

Tech-related shortage occupations include:
- Programmers and software developers
- Data analysts
- IT architects

*List updated periodically - check current version*

## Sponsor License
Employers must:
- Hold valid Sponsor License
- Issue Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)
- Meet ongoing compliance requirements

Applicants should verify employer is licensed at gov.uk

## Duration & Extension
- Initial grant: Up to 5 years
- Extensions: Unlimited (while meeting requirements)
- No maximum stay under this route

## Path to Settlement (ILR)
After 5 continuous years on Skilled Worker visa:
1. Still employed by licensed sponsor
2. Meet salary threshold (going rate or £26,200)
3. Pass Life in the UK test
4. Meet English language requirement (B1)
5. Not have excessive absences (180+ days/year)

ILR allows:
- Unlimited work for any employer
- Access to public funds
- Path to citizenship after 12 months

## Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
- £1,035/year for adults
- £776/year for children/students
- Must be paid upfront for visa duration
- Provides NHS access

## Application Costs (2024)
| Category | Fee |
|----------|-----|
| Application (up to 3 years) | £719 |
| Application (over 3 years) | £1,420 |
| Priority service | £500 |
| Super Priority (1 day) | £1,000 |
| Health surcharge | £1,035/year |

## Processing Times
- Standard: 3-8 weeks
- Priority (outside UK): 5 working days
- Super Priority (inside UK): 1 working day

## Switching & Dependents

### Switching From Other Visas
Can switch from: Student, Graduate, other work visas
Cannot switch from: Visitor, short-term visas

### Dependents
- Spouse/partner: Same visa duration, full work rights
- Children under 18: Same visa duration
- Each dependent pays own fees + IHS

## Key Considerations

### Remote Work
- Must work for sponsor primarily
- Some remote work acceptable
- Cannot work primarily for overseas parent company

### Second Jobs
- Allowed if in same occupation code
- Up to 20 hours/week additional
- Must be at same skill level

### Changing Employers
- Must get new CoS from new employer
- Apply for visa variation
- Cannot start until approved (inside UK) or visa issued (outside)

## Common Rejection Reasons
- Sponsor license issues
- Salary below threshold
- Job doesn't match skill level
- English language evidence insufficient
- Maintenance funds issues (if applicable)

## Global Talent Alternative
For exceptional individuals, Global Talent visa offers:
- No job offer required
- No English requirement
- No minimum salary
- Faster settlement (3 years if exceptional talent)

Requires endorsement from Tech Nation (for tech)
